# VocalMind Agent Guide & Manual

This guide describes the dashboard features and call-coaching workflows available to users with the **Agent** role in the VocalMind platform.

---

## 1. Access Control & Privacy (RBAC)

Agents have restricted data visibility compared to managers:
*   Agents can **only** see and play back calls assigned to them.
*   Agents cannot access the AI Manager Assistant, company policy uploads, or the organization settings panel.

---

## 2. Agent Dashboard & Performance

The **Agent Dashboard** provides performance feedback:
*   **Averages**: Displays your personal Empathy, Compliance, and Resolution averages.
*   **Weekly Trends**: Visualizes score patterns over recent weeks.
*   **Recent Calls**: Lists your latest processed call recordings.

---

## 3. Call Coaching Detail View

Clicking a call opens the **Call Coaching** interface:
*   **Audio Playback**: Plays back call audio, highlighting matching transcript segments.
*   **Coaching Triggers**: Displays coaching suggestions generated by LLM analysis (e.g. *cross-modal dissonance*, indicating a mismatch between your polite words and a tense acoustic tone).
*   **SOP Adherence Steps**: Shows which required resolution steps you completed and which ones you missed.

---

## 4. Emotion Dispute Workflow

If you believe the acoustic emotion model incorrectly classified a segment of your call (e.g., classifying a neutral response as "angry", or a polite greeting as "frustrated"):

1.  Open the call detail page and find the incorrect utterance turn.
2.  Click the **Dispute** button.
3.  Enter an explanation note (e.g., *"I was speaking calmly and searching the database, not sounding frustrated"*).
4.  Submit the dispute. This sets the event's status to `is_flagged = TRUE`, routing it to the **Manager Review Queue**.
5.  Once the manager reviews and corrects the label, the corrected data is logged.
6.  **Retraction**: You can delete/retract your dispute at any time before it is reviewed.
